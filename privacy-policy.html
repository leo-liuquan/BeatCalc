BeatCalc Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-04
Applies to: BeatCalc (the “App”)

This Privacy Policy explains how BeatCalc handles information when you use the App on your device.

The App is designed to minimize data collection and does not require you to create an account.

For related Google Play guidance, see Privacy, Deception and Device Abuse (Play Console Help).

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or upload personally identifiable information through the App.

To provide the calculation history feature, the App stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Calculation content: expressions you enter and the results generated by the App
  • Date information: a date value used to group and display history items

This data is stored inside the App’s private storage (app sandbox) using a local database.

2. How We Use Information

Local history data is used only to:

  • display your calculation history inside the App
  • allow you to clear history when you choose to do so

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

The current version of the App does not include third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, or crash reporting that collect your data.

4. Permissions and Network Access

The App does not require access to sensitive device features (such as contacts, location, camera, microphone, or SMS) to provide core functionality.

The App is intended to work offline and does not request the INTERNET permission in the current configuration.

5. Data Storage, Retention, and Deletion

  • Storage: local storage on your device
  • Retention: kept until you clear history or uninstall the App
  • Deletion: you can delete your history from within the App using the clear history option

6. System Backups (May Occur)

The App enables Android backup related settings (for example, allowBackup). Depending on your device and system settings, your App data (including calculation history) may be included in system backups or device-to-device transfers.

Such backup/transfer behavior is controlled by the Android system and your device configuration, and is not initiated by the App itself.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to keep your data within local storage. However, no method of storage is 100% secure. If your device is compromised (for example, by malware or unauthorized access), your local data may be at risk.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the effective date at the top when changes are made.
