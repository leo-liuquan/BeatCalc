## BeatCalc Privacy Policy

### Summary (English)

- **Local data**: Calculation history is stored locally on your device (inside the App sandbox).
- **Third-party services**: This App uses **Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK)** to display ads, and it may include **affiliate/referral links** that open third-party websites or stores.
- **Network permissions**: To load ads and open external links, the App may use network-related permissions such as `INTERNET` and `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`.
- **Third-party privacy policies**: Ads and external links are handled by third parties under their own privacy policies.

---

**Effective date**: 2026-02-09  
**Scope**: This Privacy Policy applies to BeatCalc (the “App”) and explains how information is handled when you use the App on your device.

This App follows Google Play guidance related to privacy and deceptive behavior (see: [Privacy, Deception and Device Abuse](https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/topic/9877467)).

### 1. Information We Collect

The App does **not** require you to create an account.

- **App features (calculation history)**: The App does **not** actively collect or upload your calculation history or other personally identifiable information. Calculation history is stored locally on your device.
- **Third-party services**: The App uses third-party services (for example, Google AdMob) that may collect certain information as described in their own privacy policies (see Section 3).

To provide the **calculation history** feature, the App stores the following information **locally on your device**:

- **Calculation content**: the expression you enter (expression) and the calculated result (result)
- **Date information**: a date value used to group and display history items (date)

This information is stored only within the App’s private local storage (app sandbox) using a local database.

### 2. How We Use Information

Local calculation history is used only to:

- **Display history in the App** (so you can review and reuse past calculations)
- **Support clearing history** (only when you choose to do so)

### 3. Sharing of Information

#### 3.1 Data you store in the App

**We do not share your calculation history with third parties.**  
We do not sell, rent, or otherwise disclose your calculation history to third parties.

#### 3.2 Third-party services used by the App

The App may use the following third-party services:

- **Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK)**: Used to display ads (including app open ads). AdMob may collect information such as **Advertising ID**, **IP address**, **device identifiers**, and **ad interaction data** to provide, personalize, and measure ads, depending on your device settings and Google/AdMob configuration.  
  - AdMob/Google privacy policy: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  - More info on ads: [Google: How advertising works](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

- **Affiliate / referral links (external links)**: The App may contain links that take you to third-party sites or stores. If you click an affiliate/referral link, the third party may receive referral information and may collect data according to its own privacy policy. We do **not** receive your payment details; purchases and related data (if any) are handled by the third party.

### 4. Permissions and Device Access

The App does not need access to contacts, location, camera, microphone, SMS, or other sensitive information to provide core functionality.

Depending on the build/version, the App may request:

- `INTERNET` and `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`: to load and display ads (AdMob) and to open external/affiliate links.

### 5. Data Storage, Retention, and Deletion

- **Storage location**: local storage (inside the App sandbox)
- **Retention**: kept until you clear history in the App or uninstall the App
- **Deletion**: you can delete stored history using the “Clear history” option in the App

#### Android System Backups (May Occur)

The App enables Android backup-related configuration (for example, `allowBackup`). Depending on your device and system settings, **your App data (including calculation history) may be included in system backups or device-to-device transfers**. This behavior is controlled by the Android system and your device configuration, and is not initiated by the App itself.

### 6. Security

We take reasonable measures to keep data limited to local storage. However, no software can guarantee absolute security. If your device is compromised (for example, rooted/jailbroken, infected by malware, or accessed by an unauthorized person), your local data may be at risk.

### 7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not primarily directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

### 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when App functionality or data handling changes. We will update the effective date above when changes are made.
